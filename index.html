About Joseph

Painter & Visual Artist • Based in New Jersey

I’m Joseph Horn, an artist who loves the way color can hold a feeling. I paint and draw to capture motion, music, and memory — the way city lights smear across a rainy window, the quiet of early mornings, the warmth of familiar faces. My work lives where intuition meets structure: bold shapes, layered textures, and rhythms that feel like jazz.

Inspiration finds me in everyday rituals: sketching on the train, studying shadows on a sidewalk, or listening to old records while the paint dries. I’m fascinated by how small moments become stories — and how those stories become art.

FAQ

How do we start a commission? Send a note via the contact form with your idea, preferred size, and timeline. I’ll respond with a proposal and next steps.
What mediums do you work in? Acrylics, charcoal, ink, and mixed media on canvas or paper.
Can I request revisions? Yes — we’ll align on the concept upfront. A round or two of refinements is typical for commissioned paintings.
Do you ship? Yes, securely packaged and insured. Shipping costs depend on size and destination.
Do you work outside New Jersey? Absolutely — I work with clients worldwide.
